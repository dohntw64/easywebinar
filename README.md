**The Evergreen Webinar Strategy: Does It Work?**

Let's address the elephant in the room: automated evergreen webinars. Some marketers swear by them. Others call them manipulative.

Here's the reality: evergreen webinars work when done ethically. The key is transparency. If you're pretending something is live when it's not, that's dishonest. But if you're upfront that it's a recorded presentation available on-demand, while still creating structure and urgency? That's just good marketing.

EasyWebinar lets you choose your approach. You can be fully transparent ("Watch our pre-recorded training now") or use simulive elements that blur the line. My take: err on the side of transparency. People aren't stupid, and they appreciate honesty more than fake urgency.

What makes evergreen webinars valuable is the 24/7 sales potential. A well-crafted automated webinar can generate leads and sales while you sleep. I've seen case studies of six-figure launches from single evergreen webinars running for months.

The formula typically involves:
1. A registration page with clear value proposition
2. A 45-60 minute presentation teaching something valuable
3. A pitch for your product/service in the last 15 minutes
4. A time-limited offer that expires after the webinar
5. Follow-up email sequences for both buyers and non-buyers

EasyWebinar handles all five steps in one platform. Whether you use them ethically or sleazily is up to you.
